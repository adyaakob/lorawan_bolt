LoRaWAN Worker Tracking Documentation

Comprehensive evaluation and implementation guide for real-time worker tracking in oil palm plantations.

Project Overview

Contractor: TEQ Armada Sdn Bhd.

Client: Sabah Softwoods Berhad

Location: Oil palm plantation estate in Sabah

Equipment: Dragino Tracker D devices & DL0S86 gateway

Duration: 7 weeks

Budget: RM33,695 - RM42,325

TEQ Armada's comprehensive evaluation of LoRaWAN worker tracking system, focusing on overcoming dense foliage challenges and optimizing plantation operations for enhanced worker safety.

Key Objectives

  • • LoRaWAN Coverage: Evaluate signal penetration in dense plantation environments
  • • GPS Accuracy: Test location tracking under tree canopies
  • • Device Performance: Assess Dragino Tracker D functionality and battery life
  • • Cellular Connectivity: Analyze backhaul signal strength for gateway placement
