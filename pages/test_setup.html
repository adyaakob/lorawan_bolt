LoRaWAN Field Test Setups

This report details the test setups and procedures for evaluating the LoRaWAN-based worker tracking system in a field environment, specifically within an oil palm plantation in Sabah, Malaysia. This evaluation will utilize three Dragino Tracker D end devices and one Dragino DL0S86 gateway.

bullseye Project Objectives

  • cell_tower

    Network Coverage Assessment

    Determine how effectively LoRaWAN signals can penetrate dense oil palm tree foliage and reach tracker devices across different plantation areas. This assessment is crucial for understanding signal propagation characteristics in the challenging plantation environment.

  • satellite_alt

    GPS Accuracy Evaluation

    Assess the precision of GPS location tracking under dense canopy conditions where satellite signals may be obstructed. This evaluation is essential for ensuring reliable worker position tracking throughout the plantation.

  • memory

    Device Performance Testing

    Evaluate the Dragino Tracker D devices' overall functionality, including data transmission rates, communication latency (time for data to travel from device to gateway), and battery life under typical plantation usage conditions.

  • signal_cellular_alt

    Backhaul Connectivity Analysis

    Measure and map cellular signal strength across the plantation to identify optimal gateway placement locations for reliable internet connectivity and data backhaul. This is critical for ensuring consistent data transmission to central monitoring systems.

These objectives are crucial for determining the feasibility and effectiveness of the LoRaWAN-based worker tracking system in the specific context of oil palm plantations. The evaluation results will provide valuable data to optimize network deployment, assess device suitability, and identify potential challenges for full-scale implementation.

Test Setup 1: Line-of-Sight Testing

Objective

Evaluate LoRaWAN performance in an open area with clear line of sight between gateway and end devices.

Procedure

  • Deploy gateway in central, elevated location
  • Position trackers at 100m, 500m, and 1km
  • Ensure minimal obstructions
  • Record RSSI, SNR, and packet loss
